  • About georgia

    • History of georgiaGeorgia is a country whose history dates back to prehistoric times. It has a rich cultural heritage, evident in numerous historical landmarks, including the beauty of medieval churches. Cultural centers located in Georgia were considered significant trade and cultural hubs during the Middle Ages.

    Culture and TraditionsIn Georgian culture, folk traditions, music, and the variety of national dishes hold a special place. Georgian hospitality and wine culture are vital parts of our cultural identity. Additionally, Georgian weddings and national celebrations reflect the beauty and depth of our traditions.

    Kings of Georgia

    The history of Georgia is rich with great kings who developed and strengthened the country throughout different eras. King David the Builder (1089-1125) was one of the most prominent rulers, known for unifying the country and leading a cultural revival. Queen Tamar (1184-1213), Georgia's first female monarch, symbolized the Golden Age of Georgia, during which the kingdom expanded both territorially and culturally. King George the Brilliant and other monarchs played heroic roles in defending and advancing the nation.
